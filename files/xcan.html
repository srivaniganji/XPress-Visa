Study in Canada

Canada spends more per capita on education than many other countries in the world making it the study destination for students across the globe. Almost 2 lakh students get admissions in universities in Canada every year.

Why Study in Canada

Boasting some of the best universities, Canada is undoubtedly one of the most popular study abroad destinations globally. Thousands of students choose to study in Canada due to their world-class education, high academic standards in universities, and international student friendliness. Canada is consistently ranked as one of the best countries for quality of life. Also, it is considered as one of the top study abroad destinations for quality academics after Study in the USA. The cost of education and living in Canada is quite less when compared to other developed countries. And, this is wh y Canada is one of the favorite foreign education destinations.

How much does it cost to study in Canada?

Studying abroad can be expensive. However, studying in Canada has proved to be one of the most cost-effective abroad studies destinations. The fees may vary depending on the university you choose. Students willing to study abroad in Canada can expect,

Up to CAD $25,000 per year Undergraduate Degree
Up to CAD $30,000 per year Master’s Degree
Up to CAD $20,000 per year Doctoral Degree
Up to CAD $40,000 per year MBA Degree

Living expenses will depend on the location you prefer and the type of accommodation that suits you, for example, private, homestay, or on-campus housing. Most universities allow payment plans to ensure that students who study in Canada can afford their studies.
